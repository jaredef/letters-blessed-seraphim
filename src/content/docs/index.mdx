## The Present Collection of Writings

This collection of writings comprises the heretofore known letters penned by Blessed Seraphim of Platina addressed to contemporary correspondents.

The majority of the present collection consists of the 328 letters published [online](https://proza.ru/2016/10/04/1512) in Oct. 2016. Additional letters have been interpolated from Fr. Alexey Young's corpus of excerpts published in *Letters from Fr. Seraphim*.

While the index of letters herein remains consistent with the corpus of 328, those letters which have been interpolated from Fr. Alexey Young's volume of excerpts are appended with a period and further enumeration. Footnotes have been added to the letters in places where additional context might prove useful. Some headings provided in the collection of 328 are augmented to reflect a concise, abbreviated title.
